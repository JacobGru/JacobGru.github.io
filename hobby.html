My Hobby is Riding my Bike

im going to show you a step by step tutorial of how to ride one

Step 1: Get Comfortable With Your Bike

1. Walk next to your bike to feel its weight.

2. Practice getting on and off the bike.

3. Sit on the seat and balance while keeping both feet on the ground.

Step 2: Learn to balance

1. Sit on the bike and push yourself forward with your feet like a scoot or glide.

2. Lift your feet for a second or two while moving and feel how the bike balances.

3. Try to glide/scoot longer each time and keep your eyes looking ahead, not at your feet.

Step 3: Add pedaling

1. Put one pedal in the 2 oâ€™clock position or slightly horizontal/forward.

2. Place your foot on that pedal, and push down hard to start moving.

3. As the bike moves, put your other foot on the opposite pedal and keep pedaling smoothly and keep your body relaxed and eyes forward.

Step 4: Practice braking

1. Squeeze both brake levers gently and evenly to slow down.

2. Avoid pulling only the front brake though it can make you tip forward.

3. Practice stopping smoothly and putting one foot down when you stop.

Step 5: Practice turning

1. Start with wide, gentle turns.

2. Lean slightly into the turn and do not use your whole body.

3. Keep pedaling slowly and look where you want to go.

Step 6: Build confidence

1. Practice starting, stopping, turning, and riding in a straight line.

2. Ride on small slopes or grass to improve balance and control.

3. Gradually try new environment like bike paths and quiet roads.

Final Tips

-Do not rush, everyone learns at their own pace.

-Keep your eyes ahead and your bike goes where you look.

-Smile! Relaxing helps your balance more than you would think.
